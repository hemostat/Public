1 Set up

  • SNPs with MAF <1% are excluded
  • LD is estimated from the Harvard reference panel (11, 720 individuals; ~80% SNP coverage and 100% coverage for SNPs available in all 6 cohorts)
  • Data pre-processing
    • 1Mb window arroun the top SNP from GWAS
    • Estimated N for every SNP based on cohort N; the effective sample size Neff = 4 / (1/Ncasesx + 1/Ncontrols)
  • Fine-mapping in two steps; see (Mahajan et al. 2018)
    • GCTA-COJO to find index snps per locus (default settings)
    • ABF to compute posterior prob. and 95% credible sets
